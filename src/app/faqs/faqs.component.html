Frequently Asked Questions

What is BuddyRide?

BuddyRide is a smart carpooling platform that connects drivers and passengers traveling on the same route to reduce cost, traffic, and pollution.

How does BuddyRide work?

Sign up, post a ride (if you are a driver), search rides (if you are a passenger), book the ride, travel together, and rate each other after the ride.

Is BuddyRide free to use?

Yes! Creating an account, posting rides, and searching rides is completely free. Only shared trip cost is paid to the driver.

Is carpooling safe on BuddyRide?

BuddyRide ensures safety through verified profiles, in-app chat, rider ratings, and transparent trip details.

How do I book a ride?

Passengers search for rides by entering source and destination, choose the perfect ride, then request to join.

How do drivers post rides?

Drivers enter trip details like starting point, destination, date, time, and available seats, then publish the ride.
